Lawrence of Arabia(1962)

The semi-biopic of T.E Lawrence and his time in taking over Turkish occupied Arabia. Lawrence is shown to be an above average man with a certain charm that wins the people over. Leading an army to take over parts of Turkish occupied Arabia. With the stilling images of the desert that the director David Lean has put in his static shots, this is a staple and apex of cinematic storytelling and cinematography. An adaption of the book "The Seven Pillars of Wisdom" by the real T.E Lawrence, the story follows a linear structure that starts with the death of T.E Lawrence from the very beginning. Often, Lawrence is shown to be very eccentric and charismatic to the point that you can see that he's very different from the men around him.
